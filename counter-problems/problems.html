How to prevent counterfeit

Bitcoin has its own way prevent counterfeit

  • In the Bitcoin system, counterfeit happens when a person trying to overwrite the record of the transaction
  • Solution: Bitcoin protocol is peers only performing the longest blockchain.
    • When people are mining the blockchain, sometimes there might be a fork in the blockchain. The fork will stop when a peer solved the HASH function and add the transaction(block) to the Blockchain.
  • After a new blockchain is connected to the chain, All the people realize that it's the longest chain and jump back to the mainchain.

51% Attack

majority of people manipulating the system

  • A group of people that could manipulate the whole Bitcoin system if they're more powerful than the rest of their peers
    • They could stay on the shorter chain and keep adding blocks to the shorter chain and out compete the longest chain.

The older the block is, it means the more blocks need to add to make it longer. the newer the block is, it means the fewer blocks need to add to make it longer. That means newer blocks are easier to overwritten compare to the older block.
